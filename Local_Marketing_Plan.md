# Local Marketing Plan: 10 Targeted Sub-Niches for MOO

This document outlines a targeted local marketing strategy focusing on 10 specific, high-potential sub-niches. These niches were selected for their combination of high ticket values, visual potential, or reliance on local trust—all areas where "Marketing Options Online" (MOO) services can provide immediate, tangible value.

---

## Strategic Overview

**Goal:** Penetrate less saturated local markets by offering hyper-relevant "foot-in-the-door" value that leads to retainer services.
**Core Approach:** Lead with value (Education/Audit) -> Build Trust -> Pitch Core Service.

---

## Niche 1: Ceramic Coating & Auto Detailers
*High Ticket ($1k+), Consumer Passion, Visual*

### 1. Recommended Service: Website Design
**Why:** This is a high-ticket service. Customers deciding to spend $1,500+ on coating want to see a premium, slick, fast-loading portfolio. Many detailers have great Instagrams but amateur Wix/GoDaddy sites that kill trust. A "Showroom Quality" website justifies their high prices.

**The Value Add (Foot-in-the-Door):**
*   **Asset:** A **"High-Speed Portfolio Mockup"**. Take 3-4 of their best Instagram photos and put them into a sleek, fast-loading, dark-mode landing page mockup.
