Common Questions

Local SEO FAQs

Answers to questions local business owners ask about SEO.

SEO is a long-term strategy, not overnight magic. Most businesses start seeing improvements in rankings within 3-6 months, with significant results typically appearing by 6-12 months. The timeline depends on your competition, current website condition, and how aggressively we pursue your strategy.
Local SEO focuses specifically on helping your business appear in location-based searches and Google Maps. While regular SEO targets broader keywords, local SEO optimizes for "near me" searches, city-specific terms, and the Google Local Pack. For businesses serving a specific geographic area, local SEO is essential.
While you can rank in Google Maps with just a Google Business Profile, having a website dramatically improves your overall visibility and conversions. A website gives you more opportunities to rank for keywords, provides a place to send traffic, and builds credibility. We recommend having at least a basic website for best results.
Reviews are a major ranking factor for local SEO. 88% of consumers trust online reviews as much as personal recommendations. Google considers the quantity, quality, and recency of reviews when ranking businesses. We help you develop a strategy to earn more positive reviews and respond appropriately to all feedback.
No ethical SEO company can guarantee specific rankings—Google's algorithm considers hundreds of factors and changes constantly. What we can guarantee is implementing proven best practices, transparent reporting on your progress, and a commitment to continuously improving your visibility. Be wary of anyone promising guaranteed #1 rankings.
