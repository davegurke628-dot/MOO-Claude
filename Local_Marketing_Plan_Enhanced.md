## Part 1: Improvements to Original 10 Niches

### Niche 1: Ceramic Coating & Auto Detailers
*Original Service: Website Design*

**2025 Market Update:**
- The U.S. auto detailing industry is projected to reach $20.2 billion in 2025
- Ceramic coatings have entered their "3.0 generation" with some lasting up to 10 years
- Google searches for "hydrophobic ceramic coatings" spiked sharply in late 2024 and remain elevated

**Suggested Improvements:**

1. **Add Subscription Model Upsell:** Subscription-based car care is trending. Position website design to include recurring service booking integration (monthly detailing plans).

2. **Highlight Eco-Friendly Angle:** 2025 consumers expect eco-responsibility. If detailers offer water-based, non-toxic coatings, help them market this differentiator.

3. **Enhanced Email Subject Lines:**
   - "Your $1,500 coating deserves a $1,500 website"
   - "Graphene coatings are trending—is your site ready?"

4. **Additional Value-Add Asset:** Create a "Ceramic Coating 101" explainer video script they can film and post. Educational content about coating benefits drives adoption and builds trust.

5. **Tiered Service Pitch:** Offer to build service package pages (Basic Detail → Full Correction → Ceramic Pro) that upsell customers automatically.
