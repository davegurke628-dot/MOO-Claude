Common Questions

Content Creation FAQs

Answers to questions local business owners ask most.

Consistency matters more than volume. For most local businesses, 2-4 blog posts per month plus regular social content creates meaningful momentum. Companies that publish weekly see 3.5x more conversions than monthly publishers. We help you find a sustainable pace that delivers results without overwhelming your business or budget.
We start with in-depth interviews to understand your business, expertise, and how you talk to customers. For ongoing content, we use a combination of brief calls, questionnaires, and review processes. Many clients are surprised how little of their time it takes—usually 30-60 minutes per month. The result is content that sounds like you, not generic marketing speak.
Content marketing is a long-term strategy. Social content and email can generate engagement quickly, but SEO-driven blog traffic typically takes 3-6 months to build. The good news: once content starts ranking, it keeps generating traffic for years. Think of it as building an asset, not running a campaign. We set realistic expectations and track progress monthly.
Content marketing works for virtually every industry—if your customers search for information before buying or hiring, content can reach them. Whether you're a plumber answering "how to fix a leaky faucet," a lawyer explaining "what to do after a car accident," or a restaurant sharing recipes, there are content opportunities. We help identify the specific topics your audience cares about.
AI is a powerful tool, but it works best when combined with human expertise. We use AI to speed up research, generate ideas, and create first drafts—but every piece is refined by human writers who ensure accuracy, add your unique perspective, and maintain quality Google rewards. The result is content that's efficient to produce but genuinely valuable, not generic AI fluff.
